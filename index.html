About Us

The TENS - Movement Lab

The TENS - Movement Lab is Salem's most innovative fitness destination, nestled in the prestigious Royal Hall at Windsor Castle Hotel on Rajaji Road. We are not just a gym — we are a movement laboratory where every session is designed to optimize your body's performance, mobility, and strength. With a perfect 5-star rating, our commitment to excellence speaks through every workout we deliver.

Founded with a passion for functional fitness and holistic well-being, The TENS - Movement Lab brings cutting-edge training methodologies to Salem, Tamil Nadu. Our expert trainers guide you through personalized programs that focus on real movement patterns, injury prevention, and long-term health. Whether you are a beginner stepping into fitness for the first time or an athlete pushing your limits, our lab is equipped to elevate your potential six days a week.

  • Perfect 5-Star Rating — Trusted by Salem's fitness community for exceptional results and personalized care
  • Premium Location at Windsor Castle Hotel, Rajaji Road — Train in Salem's most prestigious fitness environment
  • Flexible Dual-Shift Hours (6–12 AM & 4–8 PM) — Convenient morning and evening batches available six days a week
